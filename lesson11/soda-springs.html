Corrigan Park

Corrigan Park, City Park as it is commonly referred to, is located in downtown Soda Springs right alongside Highway 30. At 11 1/2 acres it is the city's second largest park. Mr. Thomas Corrigan donated this land to the city. The Lions Club, City of Soda Springs, and the citizens of Soda Springs have helped create this park.
