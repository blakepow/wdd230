Target Audience

Persona 1 - The Researcher

I need an app that will give accurate and detailed information in every country at all times. I need it to be thorough so that I know what temperature, humidity, and visibility each major city is.

Persona 2 - The Jock

I need somthing to look at so I know where good houses to have parties are. I travel the country renting Airbnbs but can't afford a rainy day to cramp my party's style. I need to know where to rent depending on the weather of that town so I can party on forever.

Scenario 1

A young man has a beautiful date 30 minutes, but he realizes they are going to see a movie at a drive in theater in his convertable. The roof of his car was recently torn off by alligators on their last date, so he must act fast if a storm is coming and reschedule his date, or she might send her gators after him again.

Scenario 2

An FHE group at BYU-I needs to know if their group activity next week can be held outside. Their FHE leader is a busy person, and does not have time to waste checking on the weather, they need an app that will take 26 seconds or less to get results.

Scenario 3

Jerry has been looking forward to seeing his friend, Tom, for many moons. Tom lives a few hours away, and hates the rain. Jerry has been aware of this since they were young lads, and if it is going to rain during his stay he needs to know so that he can plan for a later date. Jerry is about to leave, and needs to know as soon as possible if it will be raining where Tom is.

Scenario 4

After a long day, the roomie retreat to Yellowstone had come to a close and it was time for all the ladies to return to Rexburg. Since it is Fall, they need to know if there are any snow storms before they leave so that they remain safe. One of the ladies, who only wears Gucci products, insists they only check on the best weather app.

Scenario 5

A young man has gotten caught up in doomsday preparations. He needs to know when the day will come that he goes to his parents house and seals himself in their basement for protection. He needs an app that brings him current weather and accurate storm predictions so that he can know when the right time to enact his plan is.
