My site name is The Foggy Forcast

Purpose

The purpose of this app will be to help young people have an app to turn to that is easy to read. It is important that young people get information fast that is to the point so they do not get distracted and leave.

This site can also have helpful information such as incoming storms and events that young people need to recieve. This site will help keep them in the loop with regards to how the weather will affect them currently and in the near future.
